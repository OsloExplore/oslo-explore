About Oslo Explore

Our Story

Founded with a passion for showcasing Oslo's hidden gems, Oslo Explore has been connecting travelers with the city's rich history, culture, and natural beauty for over a decade. Our expert local guides ensure every tour is an unforgettable experience.

Expert Guides

Our team consists of passionate locals and historians who know Oslo inside out. Each guide brings unique insights and stories that make your visit truly special and authentic.

Sustainable Tourism

We're committed to responsible tourism. Our tours support local communities and businesses while minimizing environmental impact, ensuring Oslo remains beautiful for future generations.
